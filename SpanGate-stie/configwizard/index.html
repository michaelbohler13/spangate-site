FAQ

Common questions

Is ConfigWizard free to use? ▼
Yes — the basic Config Wizard is completely free with no account required. Pro features including the full wizard, Network Map, NetDoctor, Aruba Instant On, saved profiles and version history require a subscription at $9.99/month billed annually at $119.88/year.
Which vendors are supported? ▼
ConfigWizard supports Cisco IOS/IOS-XE, Aruba CX (AOS-CX) and Juniper JunOS across all tools. Aruba Instant On is also supported for small business WiFi setup.
Where are my saved configs stored? ▼
Your configs are stored securely in the cloud with row-level security — only you can ever see your own data. No other user or admin can access your saved profiles or configs.
How does the AI security review work? ▼
ConfigWizard builds a professional security audit prompt from your config automatically. Copy it with one click and paste into Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, Copilot or Gemini. The AI returns findings with exact fix commands. No API key required — works with the AI you already use.
Is my network data private? ▼
Yes. Your configs are only visible to you. We never sell, share or analyze your network data. When you use the AI review feature your config goes directly to your chosen AI — it never passes through our servers.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes. Cancel any time before your annual renewal date and you won't be charged again. Your Pro access continues until the end of your paid period.
What if I need help or have a feature request? ▼
Use the feedback button inside the app or email us directly. We read every message and respond to all Pro users. Feature requests from real engineers drive our roadmap.
