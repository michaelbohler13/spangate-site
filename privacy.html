01Who We Are

SpanGate LLC ("SpanGate", "we", "us", "our") is a technology company providing network monitoring software and related services. Our primary contact for privacy matters is hello@spangate.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to the SpanGate website, the Network Monitor dashboard, and the SpanGate agent software (collectively, the "Service").

02What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

Account Information

  • Email address and password hash (via Supabase Auth)
  • Timestamp of account creation and most recent sign-in
  • Acceptance timestamp for Terms of Service

Device and Network Data (collected by the monitoring agent you install)

  • Device hostnames, IP addresses, vendor, and type information you configure
  • SSH usernames and passwords you provide for configuration pull (encrypted at rest)
  • Running network device configurations retrieved via SSH
  • ICMP ping response times and packet-loss statistics
  • Agent heartbeat timestamps

Usage and Operational Data

  • API request logs (timestamp, endpoint, HTTP status — no request body content is logged)
  • Agent software version and check-in timestamps
Note: We do not collect, store, or process the contents of network traffic passing through your monitored devices. SpanGate collects only the device management-plane data described above.

03How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • Service delivery: Storing device configurations, displaying dashboard data, generating alerts.
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity and associating data with your account.
  • Security: Detecting abuse, unauthorized access attempts, or abnormal usage patterns.
  • Legal compliance: Maintaining records required by applicable law and enforcing our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

04Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using the following infrastructure:

  • Supabase (PostgreSQL): Account data, device configurations, alerts, and SSH credentials are stored in a Supabase-managed PostgreSQL database with encryption at rest.
  • Vercel: The SpanGate web application and API are hosted on Vercel's serverless infrastructure. All traffic is transmitted over TLS (HTTPS).

SSH credentials are stored encrypted. Configuration data (which may contain sensitive network information) is stored in our database and accessible only via authenticated API requests tied to your site API key.

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted transport, access controls, and API key authentication. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your API keys and account credentials.

SSH Password note: SSH credentials stored in SpanGate are never returned in any API response visible to the dashboard or external requests. They are used exclusively by the monitoring agent during configuration pull operations.

05Data Retention

  • Alert history: automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Device configuration backups: the 4 most recent backups per device are retained; older backups are deleted automatically. All backups are purged after 30 days regardless of count.
  • SSH credentials: deleted when you remove the associated device from your dashboard.
  • Account data: retained until you delete your account. After deletion, your data is purged within 30 days.

06Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure providers: Supabase (database hosting) and Vercel (application hosting) process your data as sub-processors under their respective security and privacy commitments. We do not share more data with them than is necessary to provide the Service.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of SpanGate LLC, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will provide notice before any such transfer takes effect.

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to any third party.

07Cookies and Analytics

SpanGate uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your authentication session token client-side. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

We do not currently use third-party web analytics services. Basic operational metrics (API request counts, error rates) are derived from server logs and are not tied to individual user identities.

08Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@spangate.com. We will respond within 30 days.

09California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. No opt-out is required.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any CCPA right.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at hello@spangate.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

10International Users

SpanGate is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, you acknowledge that your data will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using the Service you consent to this transfer.

We do not currently hold certification under Privacy Shield or similar frameworks. If you have questions about cross-border data transfers, contact us at hello@spangate.com.
