FAQ

Common questions

What hardware does the agent run on?▼
Any Linux machine — Raspberry Pi, VM, or desktop. A Pi Zero 2W (~$50) handles up to 50 devices. A Pi 4 (~$150) handles up to 200. A Pi 5 (~$249) handles up to 1,000. A VM or desktop is unlimited. Windows support is available via a .exe installer. Don't want to set it up yourself? We'll ship you a pre-configured Pi for a one-time $199 setup charge.
Which vendors are supported?▼
Network Monitor supports Cisco IOS-XE, Aruba CX (AOS-CX), and Juniper JunOS. ICMP ping works on any reachable device — config pulls via SSH require one of these three vendors at launch.
Are my SSH credentials safe?▼
Yes. SSH credentials are encrypted locally on your Pi or VM using your unique API key and are never transmitted to our servers. Only config text and alert data ever leave your network. Your credentials stay in your building.
How long are configs stored?▼
Configs, diffs, and alerts are retained for 30 days and then automatically purged. We don't store your network data indefinitely. Ping status is live only — not stored at all.
Can I monitor multiple sites?▼
Yes. Multi-site support is built in from day one. Each site gets its own agent. All sites appear in a single dashboard. Plus, Pro, and District plans support multiple agents and sites.
Can I cancel my subscription?▼
Yes. Cancel any time before your annual renewal date and you won't be charged again. Your access continues until the end of your paid period.
