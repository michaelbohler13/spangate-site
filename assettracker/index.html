Privacy & Security

Secure by design. No network access required.

Asset Tracker is a pure inventory tool — it never touches your network, accesses your devices, or stores credentials.

Asset Tracker is passive — it stores what you tell it. No agents, no SSH, no sniffing. Your network stays yours.
🛡️
No Credentials Stored
Asset Tracker never asks for device passwords or network credentials. It's a hardware catalog, not a monitoring tool.

Your Data, Your Control
